Case Study: Baby Food Ad Campaign - Serenity Kids


Background

The first ad campaign done in my Innovative Advertising class, advertising a brand of baby food (Serenity Kids), in print and web formats.

The ads embody a playful theme with a combination of cut-out style graphics and simplistic icons, advertising benefits of Serenity Kids' products such as their ingredients, freshness, and portability.

Assets created and modified in Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop, composited in Adobe InDesign.
