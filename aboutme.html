Welcome to my graphic design portfolio!


Here's a little about me.



I am Aeryn Yamazaki, an aspiring graphic designer and digital artist/motion graphics animator based in O‘ahu, Hawai‘i!

I am currently a student at University of Hawai‘i - West O‘ahu, where I am majoring in Creative Media with a focus in Communications and New Technologies. I have studied a variety of forms of Creative Media, ranging from Graphic Design for advertisements and website layouts to video (animated and film).

Outside of these, I am also a casual Procreate artist. I like to draw my own characters (and sometimes others) in a variety of styles– often inspired by various interests of mine.
