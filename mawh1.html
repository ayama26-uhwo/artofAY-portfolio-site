Make-A-Wish Hawaii Internship


Heart Month 2026 Instagram Posts


Background

My first project at Make-A-Wish Hawaii: A collection of Instagram carousel posts for their Heart Month 2026 social media campaign.

Its goal was to spread awareness about MAWH's referral program, specifically for eligible children with heart conditions. The posts embody MAWH's colorful and playful branding. The first five posts were posts that were uploaded throughout the month of February, highlighting five different Wish Kids with heart conditions to share information about the referral program. The final post was a sponsored version, which essentially served the same purpose but as an ad.

Vector shape assets created in Adobe Illustrator; photos modified and posts assembled in Photoshop.
